About Me


Age: 25

Hometown: Männedorf

Education: Atelier Schule Zürich Matur 2014

Hobbies: Kite surfing, Mountain biking, drums, acting

My goal as a professional Snowboarder is to become a little better everyday and push my boundaries to the maximum. I am very passionate about what I do. All year long I do everything to get better and stronger at what I do. This lifestyle consists of strength training, acrobatics, coordination and mental training. I am the most satisfied when I learned a new trick. I love Competing and showing my best when it counts. There are always ups and downs but I am fully happy and passionate about my snowboarding and very grateful to be living this life.
